Dane Williams

Co-Founder and President

Dane co-founded CodeSeed in April of 2019 with the goal of bringing more software development opportunities to Notre Dame students. Dane spent the summer of 2019 helping lead CodeSeed while also interning at Symetra in Bellevue, WA where he worked on cloud infrastructure and development. He grew up in Seattle's Eastside and now resides in the Bay Area, California when away from Notre Dame.
